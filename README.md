# Integrated-Bed-Allocation-and-Scheduling-Considering-Overflow-Capability
Patient data for "Bi-level Stochastic Optimization of Integrated Bed Allocation and Inpatient Scheduling in Dedicated Inpatient Systems with Overflow Capability"

There are two sets of data: the first is dataset for patient arrival; the second is dataset for patient LoS.
The data in row 1-10 shows the empirical distributions of patient arrival/LoS of department 1-10, where the number of arrivals begin with 1 and LoS begin with 0.
